Paper Prototypes mobile

I first chose to focus on three pages to plan my page, because these are the main pages with specific functions. In order to show a clear and loose visual effect on the mobile page, I added a folded menu bar, which integrates other information to reduce the congestion of the page.

However, I later found that the design on the left required too much programming ability, and I did not have enough knowledge to complete it, so I mainly developed the design in the middle.

In the third week, I was inspired to move dragable elements to enhance the sense of interaction. I think the page was created well, but it's a shame that part of the problem was caused by insufficient coding knowledge. For example, when you click on a draggable element with a link, it will automatically load to a new page, which is not what I want, there may be a good solution, but I can't find it.
