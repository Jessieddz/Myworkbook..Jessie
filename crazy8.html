Crazy 8

"Crazy 8" is a brainstorming activity used to inspire a design approach. Participants were asked to quickly outline eight ideas in eight minutes, each taking about one minute.

My eight minutes showed interactive themes, such as picture layout in a chain, information layout in a book, etc., to embody the concept of simplicity, but I ignored my current coding skills. I thought it was a good idea to dig deeper and create more modern, technical interactive media.
